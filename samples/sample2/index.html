Parts of the Solar System

  • Sun: approximately 333,000 times the mass of Earth, diameter of approximately 1.39 million km.
  • Planets: Mercury to Neptune, from the inner rocky planets to the outer gas giants.
  • Orbits: ellipses, nearly in the same plane as the solar wind.
